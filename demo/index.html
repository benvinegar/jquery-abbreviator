Abbreviator a jQuery plugin for making text fit into small spaces

Abbreviator is a jQuery plugin that abbreviates text nodes to fit into smaller DOM elements, with pixel-perfect accuracy, adjusting for font decoration, weight, and size across every major browser.

The Problem

Consider the case where you have tabular data, but limited column space.

Company E-mail
Johnson's Consulting Corp. inquiries@sams-consulting.com
Dynamico Engineering Group dynamico@mailinator.com

Here both company names are too long and break onto two lines. For big lists of data, this can be unsightly.

Enter: The Abbreviator

Invoke abbreviator like so:

Source 
$('td.company').abbreviate();

And it will transform the table above into:

Company E-mail
Johnson's Consulting Corp. inquiries@sams-consulting.com
Dynamico Engineering Group dynamico@mailinator.com

Notice that Abbreviator automatically wraps the shortened content in <abbr> tags, so you can see the full string by hovering over it with your mouse.
